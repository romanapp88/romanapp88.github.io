d. Payment data

If you make purchases, we may collect information necessary to process your payment (e.g., payment method details). Transactions are securely processed and stored by the respective platform providers: Apple App Store Privacy Policy and Google Play Privacy Policy.

e. Social media login data

If you sign up or log in via a social media account, we may collect certain profile information as described in How do we handle your social logins?

Some information — such as your IP address and device characteristics — is collected automatically when you use our Services. When you use our application(s), and if you grant permission, we may collect:

We also collect log and usage data and may use cookies or similar technologies to improve security, analyze performance, and enhance your experience.
